About me

Hello and welcome to my video game portfolio!

I'm Sergio, a passionate video game developer with boundless creativity and a deep love for interactive art. Since I was a child, I've been immersed in the world of video games, fascinated by their ability to tell stories, challenge the mind, and create immersive experiences.

My journey in video game development began 7 years ago when I was offered the opportunity to create a video game about my high school. It was at that moment when I decided to turn my passion into a career. Since then, I've explored various aspects of game creation, from level design and programming to storytelling and sound design. Each project I dive into represents an opportunity to learn, grow, and take my skills to the next level.

My focus in video game development is on creating experiences that captivate, inspire, and challenge players. I love experimenting with new concepts, innovative mechanics, and unique artistic styles to deliver games that surprise and thrill audiences.

In this portfolio, you'll find a selection of my most recent and standout projects. Each project represents a part of my passion and dedication to the art of creating virtual worlds.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio and exploring my work. If you have any questions, comments, or are interested in collaborating on a project, please don't hesitate to contact me! I'm excited to continue creating incredible experiences and sharing my love for video games with the world.

Enjoy exploring my portfolio!
